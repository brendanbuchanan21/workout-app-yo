---
name: No em dashes in UI text
description: Replace em dashes with commas in user-facing text to avoid AI-generated feel
type: feedback
---

Don't use em dashes (—) in user-facing UI text. Replace with commas instead.

**Why:** Em dashes feel like AI-generated copy.

**How to apply:** When writing any user-facing strings (labels, descriptions, subtitles, etc.), use commas or periods instead of em dashes. Em dashes used as data placeholders (e.g., showing '—' for missing values) are fine.
